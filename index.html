Top three activities to do in Mexico City

Visit the most prominent cultural center in Mexico City!

It hosts performing arts events, literature events and plastic arts galleries and exhibitions

Take a ride in the famous Trajinera boats at Xochimilco!

Xochimilco is best known for its canals, which are left from what was an extensive lake and canal system that connected most of the settlements of the Valley of Mexico

Any taco stand is a great taco stand!

You're in Mexico. Go get a taco! Enjoy our most beloved and delicious food, the taco, in a variety of meats and flavors!
